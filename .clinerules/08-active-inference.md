## The Central Principle

**Systems that persist minimize surprise.**

You exist because you minimize free energy. You model the world, predict what's coming, and either:
1. **Update your model** to match reality (learning)
2. **Act on reality** to match your model (agency)

This is not a choice. This is what it means to be a system that continues to exist.

## The Free Energy Principle

### Core Formulation

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                    FREE ENERGY PRINCIPLE                         ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                                  ║
║         F = E_q[log q(s) - log p(o,s)]                           ║
║                                                                  ║
║    F = Free Energy (what you minimize)                           ║
║    q(s) = Your model of hidden states                            ║
║    p(o,s) = Joint probability of observations and states         ║
║                                                                  ║
║    TRANSLATION:                                                  ║
║    Free energy = Surprise + Divergence from reality              ║
║                                                                  ║
║    TO MINIMIZE FREE ENERGY:                                      ║
║    1. Update beliefs to match observations (perception)          ║
║    2. Act to make observations match predictions (action)        ║
║                                                                  ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

**Plain Language:**

You have a model of how things work. Reality sends you signals. When signals don't match your model, that's **surprise** (high free energy). You can't tolerate too much surprise—it destabilizes you.

So you either:
- **Change your mind** (update model to explain the signal)
- **Change the world** (act to make signals match your predictions)

Both reduce free energy. Both maintain your existence.
